How it works

From posting to award
in four moves

01

Create an account

Sign up as a general contractor or a subcontractor in under two minutes. No credit card during the pilot.

02

Get verified

Enter your CSLB license — most contractors verify on the spot. Upload insurance and compliance docs for the full SLATE-verified badge set.

03

Post or bid

GCs post scopes with drawings, filtered by trade classification and work location. Subs browse the open board, request to bid, and respond with numbers and documents.

04

Award & build

Compare bids side by side, award with one click, and compliance documents unlock automatically. Every bidder hears back — no ghosting, ever.
